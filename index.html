MODERN FAMILY

SYPNOSIS

Modern Family is an american sitcom that shows us the different lives of three different yet related families(the Pritcetts, Dunphys and Tuckers) in an honest and humorous way. The show displays the troubles faced by each family and how they resolve it in their own humorous way.

CHARACTERS

The Pritchett-Dunphy Family is consisted of 5 members(Phil, Claire, Haley, Alex and Luke)Phil Dunphy, husband of Claire Pritchett-Dunphy, is the father of 3 children namely Haley, Alex and Luke. Phil is described to be a generally optimistic and fun person. He likes to refers to himself as a "cool dad" and is very devoted to his family, always finding way to be able to bond with his family. Meanwhile Claire Dunphy, wife of Phil Dunphy and mother of the three children, is described to be a perfectionist and sometimes can be "bossy" in contrast to her laid-back husband. She is also described to be competitve and will do anything to win but most of all she is a determined person, doing anything for the people she loves. Furthermore, their eldest daughter Haley Dunphy(farthest right side of the picture)was described to be an irresponsible and rebellious child although struggling academically. She has a very likeable nature and fashion sense which results to her being famous in her high school. The middle child, Alex Dunphy(with glasses), is a sarcastic character with an overachieving personality. Though being one of the youngest in the family, she is arguably the most intelligent member in the family. The Youngest, Luke Dunphy, is a playful and mishevious character who is often seen to be in his own world. Luke is also often described to have low intelligence and common sense which leads him to innocence.

Cameron Tucker(farthest right), husband of Mitchelle Pritchett and Father of Lily Tucker-Pritchett, , is portrayed to have a very bubbly and outgoing personality in contrast to his husband, Mitchelle. Cam seems to like being the center of attention and is emotionally sensitive which leads to him not taking criticism well. Mitchelle Pritchett, husband of Cameron Tucker and Father to Lily Tucker-Pritchett, is a perfectionist(much like his older sister Claire)and prefers to stay professional, again in contrast of Cam. Mitchelle is critical in nature and usually very honest about his thoughts. Lastly their daughter Lily Tucker-Pritchett is the adoptive daughter of Cam and Mitch. She is portrayed as a sassy,selfless and bubbly child which comes from both his fathers.

The last family is the Delgado-Pritchett is composed of 5 members(Jay, Gloria, Manny, Joe and Stella). Jay Pritchett, the oldest of the family and the biological father to Claire, Mitch and Joe while also being the step dad to Manny. He is typically strict and tough since he was a war veteran. Gloria Delgado-Pritchett is the wife of Jay Pritchett and mother to Manny Delgado. She is a fesity and generally a fun-loving person. She is also a very outspoken person, generally having a very loud voice. Meanwhile, Manny(Son of Gloria and step son of Jay)is described to be an adult stuck in a childs' body as he is very mature for his age. Manny also has knack to romance, literature and arts; often using the said skill to find his "true love". Joe, the youngest in the family, is portrayed to be an excited and bubbly child. Lastly, the last members is their dog Stella.
